Umberto Boccioni,1913

Dynamism of a Soccer Player

Dynamism of a Soccer Player is a 1913 Futurist oil painting by Italian artist Umberto Boccioni (1882–1916). It is held in the Museum of Modern Art in New York.

Dynamism of a Soccer Player captures the fragmented motion of an athlete in action. At the center, a powerful calf anchors the composition, while surrounding limbs dissolve into dynamic, abstract forms. The painting bursts with vibrant, segmented colors, creating the illusion of light radiating from the figure. Transparent and opaque overlapping shapes reflect the influence of Cubism, while its energy and fluidity embody Futurist ideals. In line with Boccioni’s Futurist Painting: Technical Manifesto, the work doesn't portray the figure in isolation but as part of its atmosphere—where light and motion dissolve the boundaries of the body itself.
